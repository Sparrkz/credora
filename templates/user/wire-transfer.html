Wire Transfer Help

A wire transfer is a fast, secure way to send money electronically from one bank account to another, either domestically or internationally. This method is often used for large amounts, important transactions, or time-sensitive payments.

Processing Time Transfers are typically processed within 1–2 business days. Delays may occur due to holidays or unforeseen circumstances.
Transfer Limits Daily transfer limits apply based on your account tier. Contact support for more details or to increase your limit.
Fees A flat fee applies for international transfers. Domestic transfers are typically free.
